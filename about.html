About RIE Ajmer

The Regional Institute of Education, Ajmer, is a constituent unit of the National Council of Educational Research and Training (NCERT), New Delhi. It caters to the educational needs of the states of Rajasthan, Gujarat, Punjab, Haryana, Uttar Pradesh, Himachal Pradesh, Jammu & Kashmir, and the Union Territories of Delhi and Chandigarh.
